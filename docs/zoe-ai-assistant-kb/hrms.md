# Sharp Matrix HR Management — 1st Line Support Knowledge Base

> **App name:** Matrix HR Management (HRMS)
> **URL:** `https://intranet.sharpsir.group/matrix-hr-management/`
> **Purpose:** Manage employee records, vacations, onboarding/offboarding, performance reviews, compensation, documents, and organizational structure.
> **Users:** All employees, Managers, HR Team, Finance Team, Admins

---

## What Is Matrix HR Management?

Matrix HR Management (HRMS) is the human resources app for Sharp Sotheby's International Realty. It serves as the central system for employee directory and profiles, vacation and leave management (with multi-step approval), onboarding and offboarding workflows, performance reviews, compensation tracking, document management, organizational structure visualization, and an internal social feed. Every employee uses it for self-service HR tasks; managers use it for team oversight; HR uses it for personnel administration.

---

## How to Access Matrix HR Management

1. Open your browser and go to `https://intranet.sharpsir.group/matrix-hr-management/`.
2. You will be redirected to the Sharp Matrix SSO login page.
3. Sign in with your company email and password, or use "Sign in with Microsoft".
4. After login you land on your **My Dashboard** (Welcome page).

If you cannot log in, your account may not have access to HRMS. Contact your Admin or IT department.

## Main Features

### My Dashboard (All Employees)

Your personal landing page showing:
- Welcome message with your name and a personal greeting
- **Action Items** — cards highlighting items needing your attention: Pending Documents, Reviews Due, Onboarding Tasks, Upcoming Vacation, Pending Profile Edits
- **Your profile** — identity, contact info, and organization details
- **My Manager** and **My Team** — quick view of your reporting line and org chart
- **Vacation Balance** — current leave balance at a glance
- **Recent Posts** — latest items from the Matrix People social feed
- **Upcoming Public Holidays** — next holidays for your country
- **People Search** — autocomplete search to quickly find colleagues

### People (Employee Directory)

Browse all employees in the organization.

1. Click **Employees** in the sidebar (under Organization). The page title is **People**.
2. Search by name, email, or title (minimum 2 characters). Filter by function, location, business unit, or manager.
3. Switch between **grid** and **list** views.
4. Click any employee to view their full profile.

### Organizational Structure

View the company hierarchy.

1. Click **Structure** in the sidebar.
2. Browse the interactive org chart with pan and zoom controls, showing departments, teams, and reporting lines.
3. Click any node to navigate to that employee's profile.
4. Legend distinguishes between direct reports and dotted-line relationships.
5. Stats at the top show total employees, functions, and locations.

### Matrix People (Social Feed)

Internal social feed for company updates and communication.

1. Click **Matrix People** in the sidebar.
2. View posts from colleagues.
3. Create a new post, add comments, or react to existing posts.
4. System-generated posts appear for upcoming public holidays.

### My Vacations

Request and track your own vacations and leave.

1. Click **My Vacations** in the sidebar. The page has three tabs: **Requests**, **Calendar**, and **Planner**.
2. View your **leave balance** at the top of the page.
3. To request time off:
   - Click **New Leave Request** (button labeled "New Leave Request").
   - Select leave type: **Paid Vacation**, **Sick Leave**, **Unpaid Day Off**, or **Other** (notes required for Other).
   - Select a date range using the calendar picker.
   - Add notes if needed.
   - Click **Submit Request**.
4. Track your request through the multi-step approval chain:

| Approval Step | What Happens |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Manager** | Your direct manager reviews and approves or rejects |
| **HR Review** | HR reviews policy compliance and approves or rejects |
| **Finance** | Finance processes the payroll adjustment |
| **Completed** | Vacation is fully confirmed |

The request status shows as **Pending**, **Approved**, **Rejected**, or **Cancelled** in your requests list. You can see which approval step the request is currently at.

5. You receive notifications at each step.
6. Use the **Calendar** tab to see your vacations on a calendar view.
7. Use the **Planner** tab to plan future vacations in advance (these show as "Planned" until you create a formal request).

### My Performance

View your performance reviews and goals.

1. Click **My Performance** in the sidebar.
2. See active review cycles and your review status.
3. View feedback and goals assigned to you.

### My Documents

Access documents assigned or distributed to you.

1. Click **My Documents** in the sidebar.
2. View, download, or sign documents distributed by HR.

## Use Cases: Common HR Scenarios

### Scenario 1: New Employee Joins the Company
Maria starts her first day at Sharp SIR.
1. **HR Admin** creates an onboarding checklist in HRMS → Onboarding using a predefined template.
2. Maria receives her SSO credentials and logs into the Agency Portal.
3. Maria opens HRMS → **My Dashboard** to see her welcome page, assigned tasks, and documents to sign.
4. Maria browses the **Employee Directory** to find her team members and manager.
5. Maria checks the **Org Chart** to understand the company structure.
6. Over the first week, Maria completes onboarding tasks (equipment pickup, policy reading, training sessions).
7. HR tracks completion in the Onboarding section.

### Scenario 2: Requesting a Vacation
You want to take a week off next month.
1. Open HRMS → **My Vacations**.
2. Check your **leave balance** at the top — make sure you have enough days.
3. Click **New Request** → select dates, leave type, and add a comment ("Family holiday").
4. Submit. Your manager receives a notification.
5. **Your manager** opens Team Vacations and approves your request.
6. **HR** receives the request and approves after checking policy compliance.
7. **Finance** processes the payroll adjustment.
8. You receive a notification: "Vacation confirmed."

### Scenario 3: Manager Handles Team Conflicts
Two team members request the same week off.
1. Manager opens **Team Vacations** and sees both requests.
2. Manager checks the team calendar to see who else is off that week.
3. Manager approves one request and rejects the other with the comment: "Team coverage required — please choose a different week."
4. The rejected employee receives a notification with the reason and submits a new request for a different period.

### Scenario 4: Employee Transfers to Another Department
An employee moves from Cyprus Sales to Hungary Operations.
1. **HR Admin** opens HRMS → Changes → creates a new change request.
2. Details: employee name, current department, new department, effective date.
3. The request goes through approval.
4. Once applied, the employee's profile, org chart position, and team assignments update automatically.

### Scenario 5: Performance Review Cycle
It's time for the quarterly review.
1. **HR Admin** creates a new review cycle: sets the period, assigns reviewers, and defines goals.
2. **Employees** see the active cycle in My Performance and can view their goals.
3. **Managers** complete reviews for their team members in Team Performance.
4. Once complete, feedback is visible to the employee.

## Best Practices for HRMS Users

### For All Employees
- **Check your leave balance before requesting** — avoid submitting requests you know will be rejected due to insufficient days.
- **Plan vacations early** — submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance to give your manager time to arrange coverage.
- **Keep your profile updated** — if your phone number, address, or emergency contact changes, submit a profile edit request.
- **Use the social feed** — Matrix People is a great way to stay connected with colleagues across offices. Celebrate wins, share updates, and react to posts.
- **Check documents regularly** — HR may distribute important documents (policies, agreements) that need your acknowledgment.

### For Managers
- **Process vacation requests promptly** — don't leave your team waiting. Approve or reject within 1-2 business days.
- **Check the team calendar before approving** — ensure adequate team coverage during vacation periods.
- **Provide clear rejection reasons** — if you reject a request, explain why so the employee can resubmit for a different time.
- **Review your team dashboard regularly** — monitor upcoming vacations, pending approvals, and performance reviews.
- **Use internal changes for all transfers** — even small role changes should be tracked through the formal process for audit purposes.

### For HR Admins
- **Complete onboarding within the first week** — create the checklist before the employee's start date so everything is ready.
- **Process offboarding immediately** — when an employee leaves, initiate the offboarding checklist right away to revoke access and collect equipment.
- **Keep public holidays up to date** — update the holiday calendar at the start of each year for all countries.
- **Run reports monthly** — headcount, turnover, and leave statistics help leadership make informed decisions.

## Vacation Approval Flow

The vacation process follows a multi-step approval chain:

```
Employee submits → Manager reviews → HR reviews → Finance processes → Confirmed
```

1. **Employee** submits a vacation request.
2. **Manager** receives a notification and approves or rejects.
3. If approved by manager, **HR** reviews and approves or rejects.
4. If approved by HR, **Finance** processes the payroll adjustment.
5. Employee is notified at each step.

At any step, the request can be **rejected** with a reason. The employee can also **withdraw** a pending request before it is fully processed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is Matrix HR Management?**
A: It is the Sharp Matrix app for managing all HR operations — employee records, vacations, onboarding, performance, documents, and organizational structure.

**Q: Can I see other employees' profiles?**
A: Yes, you can view public profile information (name, department, position, photo) in the Employee Directory. Sensitive HR data is only visible to HR admins.

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Go to your Profile page (click your avatar → Profile). Submit an edit request for changes that require HR approval.

### Vacations

**Q: How many vacation days do I have?**
A: Check My Vacations in the sidebar. Your current leave balance is displayed at the top.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Go to My Vacations → click New Request → fill in the dates and type → Submit.

**Q: Who approves my vacation?**
A: Your direct manager approves first, then HR, then Finance processes it. You are notified at each step.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: It depends on your manager and HR availability. You will receive notifications when each step is completed.

**Q: Can I cancel a vacation request?**
A: Yes, you can withdraw a pending request before it is fully processed. Go to My Vacations, find the request, and click Withdraw.

**Q: My vacation was rejected. Why?**
A: Check the rejection reason provided by the approver. Common reasons include team scheduling conflicts or insufficient leave balance.

**Q: My leave balance seems wrong.**
A: Contact HR. They can review and adjust your leave balance in Leave Management.

### Performance

**Q: When is my next performance review?**
A: Check My Performance for active review cycles. Your manager or HR will notify you when a review cycle starts.

**Q: Can I see my review feedback?**
A: Yes, once a review cycle is complete, feedback is visible on your My Performance page.

### Documents

**Q: I received a document to sign. Where do I find it?**
A: Go to My Documents in the sidebar. Documents distributed to you appear there with any required actions.

### Social Feed

**Q: Can I post on Matrix People?**
A: Yes, all employees can create posts, comment, and react on the social feed.

**Q: What are the system-generated holiday posts?**
A: The system automatically creates posts for upcoming public holidays to keep everyone informed.

## Troubleshooting

### Login and Access

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| **Cannot log in** | Wrong credentials or no account | Check email/password. Contact Admin. |
| **"Access Denied" on a page** | Your role doesn't have permission | Contact your Admin or HR to check your role. |
| **Team sections not visible** | You are not registered as a manager | Contact HR to verify your manager status in the system. |

### Vacations

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| **Cannot submit a vacation request** | Missing required fields or insufficient balance | Check leave balance and fill all required fields. |
| **Request stuck on "Pending" at Manager step** | Manager hasn't reviewed yet | Follow up with your manager directly. |
| **Leave balance not updating** | Balance recalculation pending | Wait for HR/Finance processing. If still wrong after processing, contact HR. |
| **Wrong number of vacation days shown** | Public holidays not counted or date calculation issue | Check the dates selected. Contact HR if discrepancy persists. |

### Employee Data

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| **My profile information is incorrect** | Data not yet updated | Submit a profile edit request or contact HR. |
| **Employee not found in directory** | New hire not yet onboarded or name spelled differently | Try different search terms. Contact HR if the employee should be in the system. |
| **Org chart looks incomplete** | Manager relationships not fully configured | Contact HR to verify the reporting structure. |

### General

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| **Page loads slowly** | Large dataset or network issue | Wait and refresh. Try narrowing filters. |
| **Notifications not appearing** | Browser or app issue | Refresh the page. |
| **Error when saving data** | Validation error or server issue | Check required fields. Note the error message and report if persistent. |
