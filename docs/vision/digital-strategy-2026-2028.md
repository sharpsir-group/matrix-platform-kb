## Mission

Sharp Sotheby's International Realty launches a comprehensive digital transformation across Cyprus, Hungary, and Kazakhstan. The strategy balances unified processes with regional flexibility, improving operational effectiveness while preserving personalized luxury service.

**Roadmap**: 7-phase implementation from January 2026 to February 2027.

## Three Strategic Markets

### Cyprus (Core Market)
- **Profile**: Foreign investors, Golden Visa holders
- **Focus**: Elite real estate, investments
- **Clients**: International investors, VIP clients
- **Segment**: Established market

### Hungary (Growth Market)
- **Profile**: Institutional investors
- **Focus**: Commercial real estate
- **Clients**: European funds, transnational companies
- **Segment**: Growth in investments

### Kazakhstan (Emerging Luxury Market)
- **Profile**: Business owners, family offices
- **Focus**: Premium real estate
- **Clients**: Entrepreneurs, ultra-wealthy families
- **Segment**: High-income deals

### Sales Process by Segment

**UHNWI Clients** (8 steps):
Direct contact/Recommendation → Assign senior broker → Meeting/Video tour → Exclusive proposals → Deal support → Contract preparation → Deal closed

**HNWI Clients** (8 steps):
First contact (site/recommendations) → Assign specialist → Consultations/Video tour → Identify needs → Qualification MQL → SQL → Personal proposals → Negotiations & contract → Deal closed

**Standard Clients** (10 steps):
First contact (site/ads/recommendations) → Lead capture → Greeting/Guides → Assign specialist → Online consultations → Needs analysis → MQL → SQL → Personal proposals → Negotiations → Deal closed

**Mass Market** (8 steps):
First contact (site/ads/social) → Auto-qualification → Automated mailings → Personalized proposals → Purchase readiness assessment → MQL → SQL → Consultations/Proposals → Deal closed

## Digital Ecosystem Architecture

The target architecture (see `vision/Sarp SIR Platform-2026-02-18-125014.mmd`) defines:

- **External Channels**: Email, Telegram, WhatsApp BSP, Voice, Google/Meta Ads
- **Own Channels**: Public website, Personal cabinet (portal), Mobile app
- **Integration Layer**: API Gateway / ESB (central hub)
- **Application Systems**: Sales Management, Listings Management, Marketing Platform, Contact Center, Website CMS
- **Data & Analytics**: Corporate Data Warehouse (CDW/CDP), ETL/Data Integration
- **AI/ML Layer**: GenAI providers, Scoring models, AI agents, RAG, Semantic Search, Recommendation engine
- **External Services**: MLS/partner listings, Financial systems, E-document management (e-sign)
- **Reporting**: BI dashboards

## Key Success Factors

1. **Strong leadership** — commitment from CDSO and CDTO
2. **Client-oriented approach** — prioritize client experience
3. **Phased implementation** — manageable stages with KPIs
4. **Regional customization** — unified core with local adaptation
5. **Continuous learning** — team training and knowledge transfer
6. **Data-driven decisions** — metrics and monitoring at every stage
